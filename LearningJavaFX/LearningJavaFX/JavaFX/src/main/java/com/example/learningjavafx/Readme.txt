The game will start, and you will see a screen with a play button and the text "Stick Hero" with a loading message.

Click on the "Play" button to enter the Stick Hero game.

In the game, press the space bar to create a stick and control the character's movement.

To pause the game and access the third screen, press the "Esc" key.

On the third screen, you will find "Resume" and "Exit" buttons.

Click on the "Resume" button to return to the game and continue playing.

Click on the "Exit" button to end the game.

Controls
Play: Click on the "Play" button.
Move Stick: Press the space bar.
Pause/Resume: Press the "Esc" key.
Resume: Click on the "Resume" button.
Exit: Click on the "Exit" button.
